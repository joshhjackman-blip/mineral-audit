OIL, GAS AND MINERAL LEASE

THIS LEASE AGREEMENT, made and entered into this 9th day of February, 2007,
by and between Sheppard Family Mineral Trust, hereinafter referred to as
"Lessor," and Devon Energy Production Company, L.P., hereinafter referred
to as "Lessee":

WITNESSETH: That Lessor, in consideration of the sum of Ten Dollars ($10.00)
in hand paid, and of the covenants and agreements hereinafter contained, has
granted, leased, and let, and by these presents does grant, lease, and let
unto Lessee the land described below for the purpose of exploring, drilling,
mining, and operating for oil and gas, situated in Hemphill County, Texas:

[legal description omitted]

3. Lessee covenants and agrees to pay Lessor as royalty the following:

(a) On oil produced and saved from the leased premises, one-fifth (1/5) of
that produced and saved, to be delivered, free of all costs and expenses
to the Lessor into the pipeline, or other receptacle to which the Lessee
may connect its wells, or the market value thereof, at the option of the
Lessor, such value to be determined by the highest price paid for oil of
like quality in the field on the date of sale.

(b) On gas, including casinghead gas and all other gaseous substances,
produced from said land and sold or used off the premises, one-fifth (1/5)
of the gross proceeds received by Lessee from the sale of such gas at the
prevailing market price.

(c) If any disposition, contract, or sale of oil or gas by Lessee shall
include any reduction or charge for the expenses or costs of production,
treatment, transportation, manufacturing, processing, or marketing of such
oil or gas, then such deduction, expense, or cost shall be added to the
market value or gross proceeds so that Lessor's royalty shall never be
chargeable, directly or indirectly, with any costs or expenses other than
its pro rata share of severance or production taxes.
